SOS Emergency Alert

Your safety is our priority. Aura's intelligent emergency detection system is always watching out for you.

Automatic Fall Detection

Our AI monitors your movements and can detect sudden falls or collapses, triggering an immediate alert.

Immediate Alert System

If a potential emergency is detected, Aura will sound an alarm and give you a few seconds to respond before taking action.

Emergency Contact Notification

If you don't respond to the alert, Aura will automatically notify your emergency contacts with your precise GPS location.

This feature acts as a virtual safety net, giving you peace of mind while you focus on your fitness goals.
